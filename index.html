About Hizchi

Hi, I'm Hizchi.

I'm a Financial Advisor with Sun Life. Can I share something that completely changed the way I look at financial planning?

Most people think insurance is about preparing for something bad. I don't. To me, financial planning is really about protecting the life you're already working so hard to build. The late nights. The promotions. The family vacations you're saving for. Your child's future. The dreams that deserve a chance to happen.

When your finances are intentional, you don't spend your days worrying about "what if." You spend them enjoying "what is."

That's why I don't believe in recommending products first. I believe in understanding people first. Where are you today? What matters most to you? What kind of future are you trying to create?

Below the video above is a short Financial Clarity Assessment. It only takes about a minute. Based on your answers, I'll personally share practical insights and, if appropriate, recommendations tailored to your goals — not a one-size-fits-all sales pitch.

Whether you decide to work with me or not, my hope is that you'll leave with more clarity than when you arrived. Because everyone deserves a financial plan that gives them confidence, not confusion.

I'll see you on the next page. — Hizchi
